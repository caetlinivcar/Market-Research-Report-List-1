<p><p>The broadband attenuator market is divided into two main types: manual and electric attenuators. Manual attenuators require operators to adjust signal levels mechanically, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for applications with infrequent adjustments. In contrast, electric attenuators use electronic components to provide automatic signal level adjustments, enhancing precision and versatility in dynamic environments. The choice between manual and electric depends on specific application needs, including signal variability, operational complexity, and desired control over signal strength.</p></p>
