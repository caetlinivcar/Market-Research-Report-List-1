<p><p>A finance lease is a contractual agreement in which the lessor provides an asset to the lessee for an extended period, allowing the lessee to use the asset while making periodic payments. Unlike an operating lease, finance leases are typically long-term and transfer most of the risks and rewards of ownership to the lessee, making it an attractive option for businesses that wish to acquire equipment without significant capital outlay.</p><p>The finance lease market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for asset financing across various sectors such as manufacturing, healthcare, and information technology.
