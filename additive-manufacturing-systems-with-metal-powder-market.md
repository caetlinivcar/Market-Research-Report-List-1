<p><p>Additive Manufacturing Systems that utilize metal powder primarily include Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electron Beam Melting (EBM). SLM employs a high-powered laser to selectively fuse metal powders layer by layer, producing intricate parts with high density and precision. EBM utilizes an electron beam in a vacuum environment, allowing for faster melting and greater material versatility, particularly for complex geometries. Additionally, "Others" encompasses emerging technologies like binder jetting and directed energy deposition, broadening the applications of metal additive manufacturing.</p></p>
