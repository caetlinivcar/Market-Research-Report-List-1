<p><p>Mechanical keyboard switches are categorized into several market types based on their action and user experience. Keyclick switches provide tactile feedback with an audible click, ideal for typists who enjoy clear keypress confirmation. Linear action switches offer smooth keystrokes without tactile resistance, favored for gaming. Soft contact switches are quieter and gentle, promoting a comfortable typing experience. Alternate action switches can register multiple inputs with a single keypress, enhancing productivity. Ergonomic switches prioritize user comfort, reducing strain during prolonged use.</p></p>
