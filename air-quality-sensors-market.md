<p><p>The air quality sensors market is primarily categorized into signal-gas monitoring sensors and multi-gas monitoring sensors. Signal-gas monitoring sensors specifically detect a single type of gas, providing focused measurements, often used for applications requiring high precision. In contrast, multi-gas monitoring sensors can simultaneously identify and quantify multiple gases, offering a broader assessment of air quality. These sensors are essential for environmental monitoring, industrial safety, and regulatory compliance, catering to various sectors from public health to environmental protection.</p></p>
