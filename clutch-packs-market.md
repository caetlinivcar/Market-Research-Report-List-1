<p><p>The clutch packs market is primarily divided into two types: single disc and multi disc. In contrast, multi disc clutches are employed in high-performance and heavy-duty applications, providing superior torque handling and smoother engagement due to their larger surface area and ability to dissipate heat more efficiently. This distinction helps manufacturers cater to specific automotive needs, enhancing performance and reliability in various applications.</p></p>
