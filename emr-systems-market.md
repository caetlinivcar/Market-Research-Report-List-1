<p><p>EMR systems are categorized into cloud-based and on-premise markets. Cloud-based EMR systems are hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, lower upfront costs, and easier updates, making them ideal for smaller practices. Conversely, on-premise systems require installation on local servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, which may appeal to larger healthcare institutions with specific requirements. Each type has its advantages, allowing healthcare providers to choose based on their operational needs and budget constraints.</p></p>
