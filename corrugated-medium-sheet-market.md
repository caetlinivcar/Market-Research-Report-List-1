<p><p>The corrugated medium sheet market comprises various flute types, each tailored for specific applications. A Flute is the thickest, providing excellent cushioning and ideal for heavy items. B Flute offers a balanced structure for lightweight products and printing. C Flute combines strength and stackability, suitable for general use. D Flute is thinner, perfect for die-cut boxes, while E Flute is even sleeker, allowing for superior print quality. F Flute is ultra-thin, providing precise packaging solutions without sacrificing strength.</p></p>
