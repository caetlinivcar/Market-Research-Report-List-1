<p><strong>The Marine Wind Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine Wind Sensor market consists of two primary types: horizontal and vertical sensors. Horizontal sensors measure wind speed and direction at a set height above the water's surface, making them ideal for assessing surface winds over large areas. In contrast, vertical sensors are designed to collect wind data at various altitudes, which is crucial for understanding wind profiles in different atmospheric layers. Both types play vital roles in navigation, weather forecasting, and marine operations, enhancing safety and efficiency at sea.</p></p>
