<p><p>Installation Work Over Control Systems (IWOCS) are vital for subsea operations, providing control over various activities like well intervention and installation tasks. The market comprises distinct types: </p><p>1. **Direct Type**: Simple, immediate control systems with a straightforward interface.</p><p>2. **Piloted Type**: Utilize a pilot signal to control larger equipment, offering greater accuracy and safety. </p><p>3. **Electrohydraulic Type**: Combine electrical signals with hydraulic power for enhanced efficiency and responsiveness. </p><p>4. **Electrohydraulic Multiplexed Type**: Advanced systems allowing multiple control signals over a single channel, optimizing communication and reducing complexity for extensive subsea operations.</p></p>
