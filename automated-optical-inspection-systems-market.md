<p><p>Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) systems are crucial in manufacturing for quality control. The market is primarily divided into two types: 2D AOI systems and 3D AOI systems. </p><p>2D AOI systems capture flat images of components to identify defects, such as misalignments or solder issues. In contrast, 3D AOI systems employ advanced imaging techniques to assess the height and volume of components, providing a more comprehensive analysis of surface features and ensuring higher accuracy in detecting faults. Both types enhance efficiency and reliability in production processes.</p></p>
