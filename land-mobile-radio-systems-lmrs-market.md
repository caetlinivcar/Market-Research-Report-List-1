<p><p>Land Mobile Radio Systems (LMRS) encompass various frequency bands catering to diverse communication needs. The VHF band (40MHz to 174MHz) is ideal for long-range communications in open areas, often used by emergency services. The UHF band (200MHz to 512MHz) offers better penetration through buildings, making it suitable for urban environments and public safety communications. The SHF band (700MHz to 1000MHz) supports high data rate applications and is increasingly utilized for advanced communication systems, including mobile broadband and critical infrastructure communications.</p></p>
