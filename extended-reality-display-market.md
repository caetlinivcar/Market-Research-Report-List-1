<p><p>The Extended Reality (XR) display market comprises various technologies, prominently featuring Liquid Crystal Displays (LCD) and Organic Light-Emitting Diodes (OLED). LCDs utilize liquid crystals and a backlight to produce images, offering cost-effective solutions with good color reproduction, suitable for various applications. In contrast, OLED displays use organic compounds that emit light individually, providing superior contrast ratios and faster response times, enhancing immersive experiences in XR environments. Both technologies cater to different consumer needs and market segments within the XR landscape.</p></p>
