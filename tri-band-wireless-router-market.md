<p><strong>The Tri-band Wireless Router Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>802.11a</li><li>802.11b</li><li>802.11g</li><li>802.11n</li><li>802.11ac</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tri-band Wireless Router market includes various Wi-Fi standards: 802.11a, 802.11b, 802.11g, 802.11n, and 802.11ac. Each standard offers different speeds and frequencies, with 802.11a operating on the 5 GHz band, providing higher speeds but shorter range. 802.11b and 802.11g operate on 2.4 GHz, offering broader coverage but limited speeds. 802.11n enhances speeds and range using MIMO technology, while 802.11ac further improves performance on the 5 GHz band, catering to high-demand applications and devices.</p></p>
